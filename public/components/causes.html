Causes

Sources of Climate Change

Climate change is a global issue that affects everyone. It is caused by the release of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere, which trap heat and cause the planet to warm.

Greenhouse Gases

Greenhouse gases trap heat in the atmosphere, causing global temperatures to rise.

Deforestation

Deforestation destroys forests, which are essential for absorbing carbon dioxide and maintaining biodiversity.

Industrial Activities

Industrial activities release large amounts of pollutants into the air and water.

Agricultural Practices

Agricultural practices release large amounts of pollutants into the air and water.

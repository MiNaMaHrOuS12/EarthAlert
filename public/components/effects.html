Effects of Climate Change

Climate change has far-reaching effects on our planet, from rising sea levels to extreme weather events.

Rising Temperatures

Global temperatures are rising at an unprecedented rate, leading to heatwaves and extreme weather events.

Rising Sea Levels

Melting ice caps and thermal expansion are causing sea levels to rise, threatening coastal communities.

Extreme Weather

More frequent and intense storms, floods, and droughts are becoming the new normal.

Ecosystem Disruption

Climate change is disrupting ecosystems and threatening biodiversity worldwide.

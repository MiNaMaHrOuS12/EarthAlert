Solutions Of Climate Change

It all starts with you. We all have a role to play in combating climate change. Here are some solutions that you can start with.

Renewable Energy

Renewable energy is a clean and sustainable source of energy that can help us reduce our carbon footprint.

Reforestation

Reforestation is the process of planting trees in areas that have been deforested.

Energy Efficiency

Energy efficiency is the process of using less energy to do the same amount of work.

Sustainable Practices

Sustainable practices are practices that are environmentally friendly and sustainable.
